SharkByte Robotics 15286

8 kids have come together to make a robotics team that completes in the First Tech Challenge. Every week, SharkByte works together to learn, make a robot, and have fun. The First Tech Challenge is a student-centered program that focuses on giving students a unique and stimulating experience. Each year, teams engage in a new game where they design, build, test, and program autonomous and driver operated robots that must perform a series of tasks. To learn more about FIRST®Tech Challenge and other FIRST®Programs, visit www.firstinspires.org
